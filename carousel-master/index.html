What is "Future with Corals"?

We are a group of teenagers trying to help the rescue of Coral Reefs, starting by making a change in the minds of the community. "Creating awareness", that is our motto.

What's the problem?See it for yourself.

The excess of nitrogen in the water comes generally, from treatment plants. Nitrogen excess shows to disrupt coral-algae symbiosis, triggering bleaching even in the absence of heat and light stress. With coral bleaching events it intensifies sea temperatures. In the other hand, corals regulate the algal growth and activity by limiting their access to nitrogen.

Importance of coral Reefs

Reefs play an important role in protecting the shoreline from storms and surge water. Coral reefs form the backbone of local economies. In many cases, tourism is associated with reefs t expanding the entire economy of a region.

More info

Our Solution

Coral reefs need low concentrations of nutrients so that their functioning is in balance. In order to achieve that we propose to implement physical-chemical and biological methods in marine waters for the elimination of excess nutrients, using microorganisms, which absorb these nutrients and create a balance in the environment.
